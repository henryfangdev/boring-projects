Windaloo App

Check current wind speed before your adventures

Windaloo presents latest wind data of nearby weather stations on an interactive map, which is useful for paragliding, skydiving, kitesurfing and other wind sports. About 40 weather stations around New Zealand have been added to this app. More stations will be added later.

Find out if the current wind direction is good for takeoff (beta)

The wind direction indicators with different colours tell the pilots if the wind direction is good for takeoff or landing.

Customise the wind speed indicators with different colours

30 km/h is not windy for you? No problem! You can change the speed colour range in the settings.
